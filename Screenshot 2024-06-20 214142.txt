The bar graph depicts the value of Eicher Motors' combined Property, Plant, and Equipment (PP&E), Intangible Assets, and Right of Use Assets over four financial years. In FY 2019-20, this value stood at Rs. 2,365.59 crores. It increased marginally to Rs. 2,423.36 crores in FY 2020-21, representing a minor growth of about 2.4%. The following year, FY 2021-22, witnessed a further, albeit smaller, increase to Rs. 2,400.63 crores. Finally, in FY 2022-23, the value rose to Rs. 2,600.28 crores, marking a more significant growth of about 8.3% compared to the previous year.
Overall Summary: 
Eicher Motors' combined assets (PP&E, intangible assets, and right-of-use assets) show a steady upward trend over the four years, indicating continuous investment in infrastructure, technology, and potentially brand building. The slight dip in FY 2021-22 could be attributed to various factors like asset write-offs or a shift in investment strategies. However, the significant increase in FY 2022-23 suggests a renewed focus on expansion and growth, potentially driven by market demand and the company's strategic initiatives. This consistent investment in assets is crucial for long-term growth and maintaining a competitive edge in the automotive industry.
