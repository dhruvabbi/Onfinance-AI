The bar graph illustrates the Net Worth of EIH Limited, measured in millions of Indian Rupees, over a five-year period from FY 2018-19 to FY 2022-23. Commencing at ₹28,551 million in FY 2018-19, the company's Net Worth demonstrated a modest increase to ₹29,192 million in FY 2019-20, reflecting a growth of 2.2%. This was followed by a marginal rise to ₹29,257 million in FY 2020-21. However, the subsequent year, FY 2021-22, witnessed a slight dip to ₹28,082 million, indicating a minor contraction in the company's Net Worth. Despite this fluctuation, EIH Limited achieved a significant surge in FY 2022-23, with its Net Worth reaching ₹31,182 million, marking an 11.1% increase from the previous year and surpassing the figures from the preceding years. This substantial growth in FY 2022-23 can be attributed to the company's strong financial performance and profitability during the year. Over the five-year period, EIH Limited achieved a CAGR of 1.8% in Net Worth.
Overall Summary:
EIH Limited's Net Worth trend reflects a period of stability followed by a strong recovery in FY 2022-23.
