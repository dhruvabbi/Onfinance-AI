The pie chart visually presents the allocation of the total economic value generated by Eicher Motors in FY 2022-23, amounting to Rs. 14,706.48 crores. Overall Summary: 
Eicher Motors witnessed substantial growth in its total economic value creation in FY 2022-23, with a significant portion retained within the community, indicating a positive societal impact. The increase in operating costs aligns with the company's growth trajectory, while the substantial retained economic value suggests efficient operations and a focus on long-term sustainability. The distribution also highlights the company's commitment to its employees, capital providers, and government obligations, showcasing a balanced approach to value creation and distribution.
