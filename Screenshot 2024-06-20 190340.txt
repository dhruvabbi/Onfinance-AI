The bar graph illustrates the Profit Before Tax (PBT) for EIH Limited, reported in millions of Indian Rupees, over a span of five fiscal years from FY 2018-19 to FY 2022-23. Starting with a PBT of ₹1,637 million in FY 2018-19, the company experienced a 33.9% decrease, resulting in a PBT of ₹1,091 million in FY 2019-20. The situation worsened in FY 2020-21, as the company plunged into a loss, reporting a negative PBT of ₹-4,431 million. This substantial loss can be attributed to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, which severely affected the hospitality sector. While still in the red, the company showed some recovery in FY 2021-22, reducing the loss to ₹-1,329 million. A remarkable turnaround occurred in FY 2022-23, as EIH Limited reported a significant PBT of ₹4,366 million. This figure not only reflects a dramatic recovery from the previous year's loss but also surpasses the pre-pandemic profit levels. Despite the volatile journey over the five years, the company managed to achieve a 5-year CAGR of 21.7%.
Overall Summary:
EIH Limited's profit trajectory mirrors the challenging landscape faced by the hospitality industry in recent years. After succumbing to a significant loss in FY 2020-21 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the company has shown remarkable resilience, achieving a full recovery and even surpassing pre-pandemic profit levels in FY 2022-23. The strong rebound in profitability underscores the effectiveness of the company's strategic response to the crisis and its ability to capitalize on the post-pandemic recovery in the hospitality sector. This positive trend positions EIH Limited for continued growth and success in the future.
