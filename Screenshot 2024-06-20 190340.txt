This graph shows EIH Limited's Profit Before Tax in the last 5 years from year 18-19 to year 22-23. EIH Limited's profit before tax (PBT) has shown significant fluctuations over the past five years.
The PBT decreased from ₹163 crore in 2018-19 to ₹109 crore in 2019-20. The company faced a substantial loss in 2020-21, with a PBT of -₹4,431 million, likely due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This trend continued in 2021-22, with the PBT at -₹1,329 million. However, the PBT recovered remarkably to ₹4,366 million in 2022-23.
The 5-year compound annual growth rate (CAGR) stands at 21.7%.
The fluctuation in profit before tax highlights the challenges EIH Limited faced during the pandemic years and the subsequent recovery.
The substantial recovery in 2022-23 shows the company's resilience and effective adaptation to changing market conditions.
