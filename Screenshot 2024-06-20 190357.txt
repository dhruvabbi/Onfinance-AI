The bar graph illustrates the Profit After Tax (PAT) for EIH Limited, stated in millions of Indian Rupees, spanning five fiscal years from FY 2018-19 to FY 2022-23. The company reported a PAT of ₹1,133 million in FY 2018-19, followed by an increase to ₹1,245 million in FY 2019-20, representing a 9.9% growth. However, FY 2020-21 witnessed a dramatic downturn as the company plunged into a significant loss with a PAT of ₹-3,431 million. This drastic change can be largely attributed to the COVID-19 pandemic and its severe impact on the hospitality industry, which forms the core of EIH Limited's business. While still facing a loss in FY 2021-22, the company managed to reduce it to ₹-1,182 million, signaling a degree of recovery. A remarkable turnaround is evident in FY 2022-23, with EIH Limited reporting a substantial PAT of ₹3,202 million. This figure not only reflects a complete recovery from the previous year's loss but also significantly surpasses the pre-pandemic profit figures. Despite the volatility over the five years, the company achieved a commendable 5-year CAGR of 23.1%.
Overall Summary:
EIH Limited's profit after tax trend clearly reflects the volatility experienced by the hospitality industry in recent years. After incurring a substantial loss in FY 2020-21 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the company has demonstrated impressive resilience, exceeding pre-pandemic profit levels in FY 2022-23. This robust recovery, reflected in the 23.1% 5-year CAGR, highlights EIH Limited's effective strategies to navigate challenging market conditions and capitalize on the post-pandemic recovery in the hospitality sector. The positive trend suggests a promising future outlook for the company.
