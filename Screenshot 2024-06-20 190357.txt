This graph shows EIH Limited's Profit After Tax in the last 5 years from year 18-19 to year 22-23. EIH Limited's profit ter tax (PAT) has shown significant fluctuations over the past five years.
The PAT changed from ₹113 crore in 2018-19 to ₹124 crore in 2019-20. The company faced a substantial loss in 2020-21, with a PAT of -₹343 crore, likely due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This trend continued in 2021-22, with the PAT at -₹118 crore. However, the PAT recovered remarkably to ₹320 crore in 2022-23.
The 5-year compound annual growth rate (CAGR) stands at 23.1%.
The fluctuation in profit after tax highlights the challenges EIH Limited faced during the pandemic years and the subsequent recovery.
The substantial recovery in 2022-23 shows the company's resilience and effective adaptation to changing market conditions.
