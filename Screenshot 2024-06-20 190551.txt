The bar graph depicts the Capital Employed by EIH Limited, expressed in millions of Indian Rupees, over a five-year period from FY 2018-19 to FY 2022-23. Starting at ₹31,015 million in FY 2018-19, the Capital Employed increased to ₹32,198 million in FY 2019-20, representing a growth of 3.8%. However, the subsequent years witnessed a relatively stable trend with minor fluctuations. In FY 2020-21, it reached ₹31,940 million, followed by a slight dip to ₹30,343 million in FY 2021-22. Finally, in FY 2022-23, the Capital Employed stood at ₹31,747 million, indicating a marginal increase from the previous year but remaining relatively consistent with the overall trend. This pattern suggests a period of consolidation and optimization of capital utilization by the company, rather than aggressive expansion or significant changes in its capital structure. Over the five-year period, EIH Limited achieved a CAGR of 0.5% in Capital Employed.
Overall Summary:
EIH Limited's Capital Employed trend reflects a strategic focus on stability and efficient capital management, particularly in the face of economic uncertainties. The relatively stable figures over the five years, with a modest 0.5% 5-year CAGR, suggest a conservative approach towards capital allocation, prioritizing internal growth and optimization of existing resources. This strategy aligns with the company's overall financial performance, demonstrating resilience and a focus on sustainable growth.
