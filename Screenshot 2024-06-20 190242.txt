This graph shows the revenue over the past five years for EIH Limited.
It shows the revenue through the years 18-19 through till 22-23
Financial analysis:
EIH Limited has had its revenue increase from 1627 Crore Rs in 2019-2018 to 1836 Crore Rs in 2022-2023. There has been a 101.6% year over year growth.
The revenue has fluctuated over the past five years reaching a low of just 474 Crores in 20-21 and reaching a high of 1836 Crore in 22-23.
A reason for this fluctuation could be the covid-19 pandemic. This caused a massive dip in revenue during the 20-21 and 21-22 years.
