The bar graph depicts the Operating Margin (EBIT) before the share of profit of Joint Ventures for Eicher Motors, expressed as a percentage, for two financial years. In FY 2021-22, the operating margin was 16.7%, represented by the red bar. This margin saw a notable increase to 20.2% in FY 2022-23, indicated by the grey bar.
Overall Summary:
Eicher Motors has achieved a commendable improvement in its operating margin (excluding JV share), rising from 16.7% in FY 2021-22 to 20.2% in FY 2022-23. This positive trend suggests better control over operating expenses alongside the growth in revenue. The increase in operating margin, exceeding the growth in EBITDA margin, indicates a possible reduction in depreciation and amortization expenses, which could be due to efficient asset utilization or changes in accounting policies.
