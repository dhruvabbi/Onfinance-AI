The bar graph presents the Earnings Before Interest, Tax, Depreciation & Amortization (EBITDA) before the share of profit of Joint Ventures for Eicher Motors, expressed as a percentage, for two financial years. In FY 2021-22, the EBITDA margin stood at 21.1%, indicated by the red bar. This margin showed a moderate increase to 23.8% in FY 2022-23, represented by the grey bar.
Overall Summary:
Eicher Motors has experienced a positive, albeit moderate, growth in its EBITDA margin (excluding JV share) from 21.1% in FY 2021-22 to 23.8% in FY 2022-23. This improvement suggests enhanced operational efficiency and profitability. However, the relatively small increase in the margin despite a significant jump in revenue from operations and JV profit share warrants further investigation into factors like cost of raw materials, pricing strategies, and other expenses. A comprehensive analysis considering these factors is crucial to assess the sustainability of Eicher Motors' profitability.
