This bar graph showcases the impressive growth trajectory of EaseMyTrip's Air Segment Revenue from FY20 to FY22, measured in millions of Indian Rupees (₹). In FY20, the revenue stood at ₹1,371.3 million. This figure witnessed a significant jump to ₹2,353.0 million in FY21, representing a 71.6% increase year-on-year. The upward trend continued in FY22, with revenue reaching ₹4,079.6 million, marking a 73.4% increase from the previous year. Overall, the Air Segment Revenue for EaseMyTrip experienced a remarkable Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 72% over the two-year period. This steep upward trend highlights the company's success in capitalizing on the increasing demand for air travel and its strong position within the online travel market.
